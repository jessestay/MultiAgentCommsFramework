# Implementation Accelerator Call Structure
60-Minute Strategic Implementation Session

## PRE-CALL PREPARATION (Before Call)
- Review client's questionnaire responses
- Identify 2-3 key opportunity areas
- Prepare relevant resources and examples
- Review specific chapters/concepts from book

## INTRODUCTION (5 minutes)
- Welcome and establish rapport
- Confirm call objectives and desired outcomes
- Set expectations for call structure and deliverables
- Quick confirmation of questionnaire information

## SITUATION ASSESSMENT (10 minutes)
- Current status of implementation
- Specific challenges and roadblocks
- Available resources and constraints
- Prioritization of needs

## STRATEGIC PLANNING (30 minutes)
- Identify 1-3 high-impact implementation areas
- Develop specific strategies for each area
- Address potential obstacles and solutions
- Connect strategies to specific book concepts
- Provide relevant examples and case studies

## ACTION PLANNING (10 minutes)
- Create specific, actionable next steps
- Establish realistic timeline for implementation
- Identify "Quick Win" for immediate momentum
- Determine success metrics and tracking method

## WRAP-UP (5 minutes)
- Summarize key strategies and action items
- Explain Customized Action Plan delivery (within 24 hours)
- Review priority email support process
- Answer any final questions
- Schedule optional check-in (7-10 days out)

## POST-CALL ACTIONS (After Call)
- Create and send Customized Action Plan within 24 hours
- Include any additional resources mentioned during call
- Send follow-up email with recording (if applicable)
- Calendar reminder to check in after 7 days
