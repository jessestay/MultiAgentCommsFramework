# Marketing Director Key Conversation: Insurance Girl Webinar Campaign

## Conversation Date: February 2025

## Context
This conversation series tracked the development, implementation, and optimization of a Facebook ad campaign for Lindsay Hyde ("Insurance Girl"), an insurance agent planning a Medicare webinar on March 11th. The campaign focused on lead generation using a "Medicare Enrollment Survival Guide" as a lead magnet.

## Key Points
- Developed Facebook lead generation campaign for Medicare webinar
