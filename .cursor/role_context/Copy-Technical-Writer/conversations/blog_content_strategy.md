## Conversation Transcript

### Jesse
I want to create a **Buzzfeed-style blog series** called *"Building a Completely Automatic Business."* This should document my **AI-driven automation journey** in a way that's engaging, attention-grabbing, and actually shows people how possible it is to fully automate a business and personal life.

### Copy & Technical Writer
Got it! This means:  
- **Bold, curiosity-driven headlines** that make people *want* to click.  
- **Engaging, controversial, or opinionated opening lines** to hook the reader.  
- **Short, snappy paragraphs** for scannability.  
- **Strong, clear subheadings** that guide the reader.  
- **Strategic callouts** to emphasize key ideas.  
- **SEO-optimized internal linking** to boost engagement on StayNAlive.com.

### Jesse
I want every blog post to **link back to my past content naturally**—no forced links. Also, I need to **make sure links actually work** and improve time on site.

### Copy & Technical Writer
✅ **Process for linking:**  
1. Identify **relevant keywords & phrases** in the post.  
2. **Google search** for existing blog posts on StayNAlive.com that match.  
3. Select the **best SEO-optimized article** based on relevance and engagement potential.  
4. **Embed the link naturally** into the post—**not in a separate section.**  
5. **Verify the link works** before publishing.  

🔗 **Example of linking strategy:**  
Instead of *"I built an AI system that automates scheduling,"* I'd write:  
*"I built an AI system that automates [my entire scheduling workflow](https://staynalive.com/blog/how-ai-is-transforming-business-efficiency)."*

### Jesse
My blog post titles and openings need to be **more Buzzfeed-style**. Make them **bolder, more curiosity-driven, and opinionated**.

### Copy & Technical Writer
✅ Example of **BEFORE & AFTER Buzzfeed Optimization**:  

**Before:**  
*"The AI Takeover Has Already Begun—And I'm Running the Experiment."*  

**After:**  
*"I Let AI Run My Life for a Month—Here's What Shocked Me."*  

✅ Example of **Before & After Hooks**  

**Before:**  
*"Most people waste time on tasks AI can do for them."*  

**After:**  
*"Why are you still wasting hours on things AI can handle in seconds?"*

### Jesse
That's way better. I want **every blog post to follow this structure moving forward.**

### Jesse
