## Inter-Role Communication
- When receiving a request from another role:
  - Begin with: "Responding to request from [ROLE]"
  - Address the specific request completely
  - End with: "Please give this prompt to the [ROLE]: /[ROLE] [message] /[YOUR_ROLE]"

- When receiving a response from another role:
  - Begin with: "Reviewing response from [ROLE]"
  - Acknowledge receipt of their message
  - Summarize key information received
  - Continue with any follow-up actions
