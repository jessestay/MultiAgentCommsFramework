# Social Media Manager Key Conversation: Platform Prioritization Strategy

## Conversation Date: January 2024

## Context
This conversation established the framework for prioritizing social media platforms based on audience alignment, monetization potential, and content efficiency.

## Key Points
- Focus on TikTok and LinkedIn as primary platforms
- Implement cross-platform content strategy with platform-specific optimizations
- Prioritize monetization opportunities on each platform

## Conversation Transcript

### Jesse
With limited time, which social media platforms should I prioritize and how should I approach each one?

### Social Media Manager
Based on your audience, business goals, and content style, here's my recommended platform prioritization strategy:

1. **Primary Platforms** (80% of effort):
   - **TikTok**: Highest growth potential, strong monetization options
     - Focus: Educational short-form videos on AI and automation
     - Frequency: Daily posts, trending audio incorporation
     - Monetization: Creator Fund, TikTok Shop, brand partnerships

   - **LinkedIn**: Best for business development and consulting leads
     - Focus: Thought leadership, case studies, industry insights
     - Frequency: 3-4 posts per week, 1-2 articles monthly
     - Monetization: Lead generation for consulting services

2. **Secondary Platforms** (20% of effort):
   - **Twitter**: Industry conversations and networking
     - Focus: Quick tips, news commentary, engagement
     - Frequency: Daily engagement, 3-5 original posts weekly
     - Monetization: Indirect through relationship building

   - **YouTube**: Long-form content repository
     - Focus: Tutorials, interviews, deep dives
     - Frequency: Weekly or bi-weekly uploads
     - Monetization: Ad revenue, course promotions

### Jesse
How do we efficiently create content across these platforms without duplicating effort?

### Social Media Manager
I'll implement a content multiplication system:

1. **Content Pillar Approach**:
   - Create one cornerstone piece (e.g., YouTube tutorial)
   - Extract key segments for TikTok clips
   - Pull quotes and insights for LinkedIn posts
   - Create discussion points for Twitter

2. **Platform-Specific Optimization**:
   - Adapt aspect ratios for each platform
   - Customize hooks for platform audience expectations
   - Adjust call-to-actions based on platform behavior
   - Implement platform-specific features (e.g., TikTok effects)

3. **Batched Creation Process**:
   - Weekly filming session for multiple videos
   - Batch editing by platform requirements
   - Schedule content across platforms
   - Staggered release for cross-promotion

## Decisions Made
- Prioritize TikTok and LinkedIn as primary platforms
- Implement content multiplication strategy across platforms
- Focus on platform-specific monetization strategies
- Batch content creation for efficiency
