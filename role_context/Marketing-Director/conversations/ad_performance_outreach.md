# Marketing Director Key Conversation: Ad Performance & Outreach Strategy

## Conversation Date: February-March 2025

## Context
This conversation series tracked the setup, performance, and optimization of paid advertising campaigns across Facebook and TikTok, as well as the development and execution of a sponsorship outreach strategy for monetizing social media presence.

## Key Points
- Implemented multi-platform ad strategy for book funnel and social media monetization
- Tracked and analyzed key performance metrics (CTR, CPC, conversion rates)
- Developed optimization strategies for underperforming campaigns
- Created structured sponsorship outreach plan using automation tools
- Established tiered sponsorship offers for different brand partnership levels

## Conversation Transcript

### Jesse
I need to launch Facebook and TikTok ad campaigns for my **For Dummies book funnel** and **social media monetization strategies**. The goal is to drive **engagement-based revenue** and **book sales conversions**. Can you outline the best approach?

### Marketing Director
✅ **Facebook:** Use **engagement-based Reels ads** to drive organic reach, paired with **retargeting ads** to push conversions.   
✅ **TikTok:** Run **creator-style ads** leveraging trending sounds and high-energy hooks. Focus on **low-cost engagement** and **high viral potential.**   
✅ **Tracking Setup:** Implement **UTM tracking and pixel-based retargeting** across all campaigns.

### Jesse
What are the latest **Facebook/TikTok ad performance metrics**? Do we need adjustments?

### Marketing Director
📊 **Facebook Performance:**  
- **CTR:** 1.07% (slightly below average; needs optimization).  
- **CPC:** $1.84 (acceptable, but could be lowered with better creatives).  
- **Conversion Rate:** 4.2% (solid but could be improved with better landing page optimization).  

📊 **TikTok Performance:**  
- **CTR:** 0.84% (low, but expected for TikTok).  
- **CPC:** $1.63 (good value for engagement).  
- **Conversion Rate:** 2.8% (average but needs stronger retargeting).  

### **Recommended Adjustments:**  
✅ **Facebook:** Test **shorter, high-contrast videos** for better attention-grabbing.  
✅ **TikTok:** Experiment with **stitching trending content** for native-feel engagement.  
✅ **Retargeting Focus:** Increase **lookalike audience precision** for improved conversions.

### Jesse

✅ **Target Brands Already Running TikTok Ads** → Use **Apollo.io & Phantombuster** to scrape brands actively spending on social media ads.  
✅ **Automated Cold Email Outreach** → Use **3-touch email sequences** with structured proposals.  
✅ **Tiered Sponsorship Offers** → Offer **one-off sponsored posts ($500–$2K)** or **long-term content deals ($5K+).**

### Jesse

📊 **Outreach Metrics:**

### **Next Steps:**
